🍌 Banana Cannon – Product Direction

Banana Cannon is not just a token launcher.

It is a real-time narrative deployment system.

The core idea is simple:

Attention moves markets.
Narratives create liquidity.
Speed wins.

Banana Cannon allows users to launch tokens manually or with Claude AI assistance. The AI scans fresh breaking news, high-velocity tweets, and trending discussions. It generates narrative coin ideas based on emerging attention spikes.

Before launch, users can:

• Preview the original tweet/news source
• Search it directly on X
• Confirm the narrative
• Copy the tweet
• Then proceed with deployment

This ensures launches are intentional and context-aware — not blind mints.

⸻

🎯 Phase 2 – Turning a Tool Into a Movement

Banana Cannon should not be a static dashboard utility.

It needs to become an ecosystem around narrative launches.

We are building:

1. Weekly Narrative Windows

Each week focuses on a specific theme to drive competition and creativity.

Examples:
• Breaking News Week
• Culture Hijack Week
• AI vs AI Week
• Utility Narrative Week
• Market Reaction Week

These themed cycles give creators a reason to participate and differentiate themselves.

⸻

2. Creator Score (Instead of Basic Leaderboards)

We will not rank tokens purely by volume.

We are introducing a Creator Score based on:

• Speed from trend detection to launch
• Engagement velocity
• Liquidity stability
• Volume quality
• Survival duration
• Wallet clustering behaviour
• Early abnormal activity

This creates a competitive layer that rewards execution, not just hype.

⸻

3. Live Discovery Board

Instead of a basic “recent launches” list, the Discovery Board will surface:

• Heating Up – tokens gaining velocity
• AI Detected – narrative-aligned launches
• Volatility Alert – unusual trading activity
• Early Narrative Plays – fresh trend deployments
• Creator Spotlight – strong executions

This prevents projects from minting and disappearing unnoticed.

⸻

4. Clawd Integration (Post-Launch Intelligence Layer)

After a token is launched, Clawd can:

• Analyse early wallet behaviour
• Detect suspicious clustering
• Flag unusual activity
• Suggest liquidity adjustments
• Identify optimal marketing timing
• Highlight volume quality vs artificial spikes

This transforms Banana Cannon from a launcher into an intelligent launch ecosystem.

⸻

🖥 UI Direction

The current UI is functional but lacks energy.

We are redesigning toward:

• Live radar-style trend scanning
• Terminal-inspired interface
• Real-time narrative feed
• “Cannon Loaded” launch animation
• Structured war-room style confirmation flow

Launching should feel like executing a strategic move — not filling out a form.

⸻

🚀 Vision

Banana Cannon is not just a mint tool.

It is a narrative infrastructure layer.

The goal is to:

• Encourage fast, intelligent deployment
• Surface high-quality launches
• Make creators visible
• Build competitive energy around attention timing
• Turn token launches into real-time events

If attention is the asset, Banana Cannon is the weapon.

We are building a system where launches don’t just happen.

They compete.
